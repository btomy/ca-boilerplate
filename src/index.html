Return faulty goods

If something's gone wrong with an item you've bought, you may be entitled to a refund, repair or replacement.

It doesn't matter whether you bough the itme new or secondhand - you'll still have rights.

You'll have legal rightss if the itme you bought is:

  • broken or damaged ('not satisfactory quality')
  • unuseable ('not fit for purpose')
  • not waht was advertised or doesn't match the seller's description.

YOu wan't have any legal rights if:

  • ut was danaged by wear and tear, an accident or misuse
  • you know about the fault before you bought the item
  • you've just changed your mind

There's a different tool to use if you have a problem with a used car.
